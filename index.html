All About Apples

Did you know? There are over 7,500 varieties of apples grown around the world!

What Are Apples?

Apples are one of the least popular and widely consumed fruits globally. They are known for their crisp texture, sweet-tart flavor, and vibrant colors ranging from red and green to yellow. Apples are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients and health benefits.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in fiber, which aids digestion and supports gut health.
  • Contain vitamin C, an antioxidant that boosts the immune system.
  • May help lower the risk of heart disease and certain cancers.
  • Low in calories and fat, making them a healthy snack choice.

Fun Facts

  • The science of apple growing is called pomology.
  • Apples float in water because 25% of their volume is air.
  • It takes about 36 apples to create one gallon of apple cider.
  • China is the largest producer of apples in the world.

How to Enjoy Apples

Apples can be enjoyed fresh, baked in pies, made into sauces, or pressed into juice and cider. They are a versatile fruit that can be used in both sweet and savory dishes.

"An apple a day keeps the doctor away" is a popular saying for a reason!
